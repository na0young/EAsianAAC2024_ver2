Welcome from the Conference Chair
The conference theme for the 3rd East Asian Regional Conference on Augmentative and Alternative Communication is “AAC Beyond Pandemic.” The theme celebrates the reopening of the East Asian Regional Conference on AAC in 2024 since we had the 2nd in 2019. However, beyond rejoicing the revival of the conference, the theme will encourage the AAC community to think and share beyond any limitations, including physical, medical or technological borders.
