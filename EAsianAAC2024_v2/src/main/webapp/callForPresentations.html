The Call for Presentations

The conference committee strongly encourage each potential participant to share their thoughts and experiences that they had in AAC before, during, or after the latest pandemic. AAC users, their family members, clinicians, researchers, and any others who are willing to learn more about AAC are all welcomed.

Please consider the following content areas for your presentations.
  • AAC clinical practice across the lifespan

  • AAC technology, design and technology integration

  • Cognition, sensory and motor aspects

  • Communication partner supports

  • Education, language and literacy

  • Equity, diversity, and participation

  • Family and consumer self-advocacy

  • Professional, ethical, and legal considerations

  • Capacity building around the world (other)

  • Other
